Welcome to Casa da Lili

This programme was designed for those who feel the need to slow down, rest and take care of themselves more consciously.

Throughout the stay, each person is welcomed at their own pace, with space for silence, nature, rest and therapeutic support. The experience brings together simple yet deeply meaningful moments: rest, nature walks, nourishing meals, mindfulness practices and therapies tailored to each person's needs.

Our Approach

At Casa da Lili, wellbeing is embraced in a simple, human and personalised way. The surrounding nature, the silence of the house, the view over the Atlantic and the unhurried rhythm of the stay are all essential parts of the experience.

Therapeutic support is offered with attentive listening, presence and respect for each person's moment. More than a fixed programme, this is an invitation to conscious care.

Casa da Lili does not follow a rigid or mass-produced format. Each stay is lived intimately, simply and adapted to the life moment of each guest.
