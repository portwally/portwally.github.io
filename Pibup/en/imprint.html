Accuracy of Property Data

The property boundaries shown in PIBUP are fetched from the open data published by BUPi (Balcão Único do Prédio) and DGT (Direção-Geral do Território). While we strive to present reliable information, we cannot guarantee the accuracy or timeliness of the data. There may be differences between the boundaries displayed and the official record held by the Portuguese tax authority, the land registry or BUPi itself.

Limited Coverage

BUPi only contains rural and mixed properties voluntarily registered by their owners. Urban areas and the Autonomous Region of the Azores are not covered. The available data represents roughly 28% of continental Portugal as of this release.
