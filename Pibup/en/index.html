PIBUP

Property boundaries on the map

Discover the boundaries of registered rural properties in Portugal. Search by parish, municipality or registration number, and view area, distance and details with a single tap.

Features

🗺

Interactive Map

Satellite imagery with the official boundaries of BUPi-registered properties overlaid. Tap a polygon to see its details.

🔍

Smart Search

Find a parish, municipality or registration number. Accent-insensitive matching, backed by an index of 2,500+ parishes of continental Portugal.

📍

GPS Coordinates

Jump directly to any coordinates in decimal degrees. Accepts comma or dot as decimal separator — European or international notation.

🔖

Saved Properties

Bookmark properties for quick access — stored locally, available even offline.

📐

Area and Distance

Computed area in m² or hectares and distance to your current position, with options to copy coordinates or open in Apple Maps.

🔗

Administrative Layers

Optionally display district and municipality borders (CAOP) to place each property in its wider territorial context.

🌎

Multilingual

Available in Portuguese, English, German and Dutch. The language follows your device settings.

🔒

Full Privacy

No account, no sign-up, no ads. Your location never leaves your device and no personal data is collected.

Coverage and Limitations

BUPi only contains voluntarily registered rural and mixed properties — roughly 28% of continental Portugal as of this release.

Urban areas such as Lisbon and Porto are largely unregistered. Urban properties and the Autonomous Region of the Azores are not covered by this app.

The data published by BUPi is anonymous: owner names are never shown.

Data Sources

BUPi

Property boundaries are sourced from the open data published by Balcão Único do Prédio (BUPi) on dados.gov.pt.

DGT / CAOP

District and municipality boundaries come from Portugal's Official Administrative Map (CAOP) published by Direção-Geral do Território.

Esri

Satellite imagery is served by the World Imagery service from Esri and its partners.
