Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Introduction

PIBUP ("we", "our", or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use the app. In short: we do not collect, store or transmit any personal data.

Data We Process

Location Data

If you grant permission, PIBUP uses your device's location solely to:

  • Show your position on the map
  • Compute the distance between you and a selected property

Your location:

  • Is processed exclusively on your device
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is not stored or logged

Saved Properties

When you bookmark a property, the information is stored locally in your device's secure system storage. This data never leaves your device and is not synchronised with any servers.

Property Data (BUPi)

Property boundaries and administrative data are fetched from the public services of BUPi and DGT. This data is already anonymised at the source and does not include owner names. Requests to the BUPi API do not include any personal or device identifier.

Data We Do NOT Collect

PIBUP does not collect, store or transmit:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Location history
  • Usage or analytics data
  • Device or advertising identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Third-Party Services

BUPi / DGT

We use the public services of BUPi and DGT to fetch property and administrative boundaries. No personal data is shared with these entities.

Esri (satellite imagery)

The map's satellite imagery is served by Esri's World Imagery service. Its use is subject to Esri and its partners' policies.

Apple Maps

When you choose "Open in Maps", the app launches Apple Maps with the coordinates of the selected property. Use of that service is subject to Apple's privacy policy.

Data Storage

All preferences, bookmarks and settings are stored locally on your device using secure system storage. No data is sent to any external servers under our control.

Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no security risks associated with its storage or transmission on our part.

Children's Privacy

PIBUP does not collect data from any user, including children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised update date.
