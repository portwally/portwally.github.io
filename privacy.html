Your Privacy Matters

Your privacy is important to us. Retro Graphics Converter does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • No analytics
  • No tracking
  • No telemetry
  • No user accounts
  • No third-party services
  • No server communication

All processing happens locally on your device

Retro Graphics Converter is designed with privacy at its core. The application runs entirely on your Mac, processing all images locally without any network communication. Your files, graphics, and data never leave your computer.

We don't know what files you convert, when you use the app, or any other information about your usage. The app doesn't have the ability to send data anywhere – it's completely offline.
