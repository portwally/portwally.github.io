Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2025

Summary: Emergency Reminder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

Introduction

Emergency Reminder ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Emergency Reminder is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not require an account or registration
  • Does not collect personal data
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not share any data with third parties

Data Storage

All data you enter into Emergency Reminder (such as household members, pets, and supply plans) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Is automatically included in your device backups (iCloud or local)

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, your Emergency Reminder data may sync across your Apple devices through iCloud. This sync is managed entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Export Features

Emergency Reminder allows you to export your shopping list as a PDF or text. When you use these features:

  • The exported file is created locally on your device
  • You control how and with whom you share this file
  • We do not receive or store any exported data

Children's Privacy

Emergency Reminder does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no data for us to provide, correct, or delete. All your data is under your control on your device. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app.
