Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 May 2026

1977 is a portal page that lists a collection of native macOS apps for retro computing. This page itself does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

About the apps listed here

Each app linked from this page (Retro Tube, BitPast, Retro TV, Retro Graphics Converter, and 1977 ASCII Art) is a native macOS application that runs entirely on your own Mac. Image and video processing happens locally; nothing is uploaded to any server. See each app’s own Privacy Policy for app-specific details.

Data we collect

None. This website:

  • Does not collect personal information.
  • Does not require an account, sign-in, or registration.
  • Does not use advertising or tracking SDKs.
  • Does not set cookies.

Website analytics

This website (portwally.github.io) uses GoatCounter, a privacy-friendly visitor counter that does not use cookies and does not track personal information. No data is shared with third-party advertisers.

Children’s privacy

This website does not collect any data, so it does not knowingly collect data from children. It is suitable for general audiences.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
