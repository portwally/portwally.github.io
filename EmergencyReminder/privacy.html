Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 31, 2026

Summary: Emergency Reminder does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

Introduction

Walter Tengler ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Emergency Reminder application (the "App") for macOS and iOS. This Privacy Policy explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Emergency Reminder is designed with privacy as a core principle.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal information.

Emergency Reminder is a fully offline application. All data you enter into the app, including:

  • Names of household members
  • Age groups and dietary preferences
  • Pet information
  • Emergency plan configurations
  • Shopping lists and preferences

is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device in the app's sandboxed container. The data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Protected by your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID)
  • Automatically backed up to iCloud if you have iCloud backup enabled for your device (this is controlled by your device settings, not our app)
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Emergency Reminder does not use any third-party services that collect data, including:

  • No analytics services
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integrations
  • No external APIs or servers

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. The only way your data can be shared is if you explicitly choose to export your shopping list as a PDF or text file and share it yourself using your device's sharing features.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device, the security of your information depends on your device's security settings. We recommend:

  • Using a strong passcode on your device
  • Enabling Face ID or Touch ID
  • Keeping your device's operating system up to date

Your Rights

Because we don't collect any data, there is no data for us to provide, modify, or delete. You have full control over your data on your device:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Modification: You can edit any information within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete plans, people, or pets within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export: You can export your shopping lists as PDF or text

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@portwally.com

Apple App Store Requirements

In compliance with Apple's App Store requirements, we confirm that:

  • This app does not collect any user data
  • This app does not track users across apps or websites
  • This app does not use any device identifiers for advertising
  • This app functions fully offline without network connectivity
