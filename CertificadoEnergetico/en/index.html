Features

🏠

Building Data

Enter the typology, year of construction, useful floor area and ceiling height. Default values pre-loaded by construction era.

📍

Location & Climate

Select your municipality. Climate data (degree-days, average temperature, solar radiation) is assigned automatically.

🧱

Thermal Envelope

Define walls, roof, floor and glazing. Typical U-values by construction era pre-loaded for convenience.

❄️

HVAC Systems

Configure heating, cooling and domestic hot water systems with efficiencies and primary energy conversion factors.

☀️

Renewable Energy

Include solar thermal or photovoltaic panels and see the direct impact on the energy class.

📊

Detailed Result

Get the energy class (A+ to F), the Ntc/Nt ratio and the breakdown by heating, cooling and DHW.

Regulatory Basis

SCE / REH

Calculation based on the Portuguese Building Energy Certification System and the Regulation on Energy Performance of Residential Buildings.

Ordinances & Decrees

Reference values per Portaria 349-B/2013, updated by Portaria 379-A/2015, and associated technical dispatches.
