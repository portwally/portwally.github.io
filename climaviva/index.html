Clima Viva

Real-Time Climate Data, Visualized

Track global warming, greenhouse gas concentrations, and climate progress with beautiful data visualizations. Explore decades of climate science — from CO₂ and methane levels to sea level rise and carbon budgets.

Features

🌡️

Global Warming Tracker

Visualize temperature anomalies from 1850 to present, energy imbalance trends, and radiative forcing components in interactive charts.

💨

CO₂ Deep Dive

Explore 800,000 years of atmospheric CO₂ history, yearly increases, emission sources, and how carbon is absorbed by oceans and land.

🔥

Methane & N₂O

Track atmospheric methane and nitrous oxide concentrations, emission sources, and understand why these potent gases matter.

📊

Climate Progress

Compare warming trajectories under different policy scenarios, track GHG emissions, sea level rise, and remaining carbon budgets.

🎨

Cyberpunk Themes

Choose from 4 visual themes — Neon Grid, Glitch & Decay, HUD Command, or Clean — to personalize your data experience.

📱

Built for iOS

Native SwiftUI app with Swift Charts for smooth, responsive data visualizations that look stunning on any iPhone.

Data Sources

NASA GISS

Global surface temperature anomalies — the gold standard for tracking how our planet's temperature has changed since 1880.

NOAA GML

Atmospheric greenhouse gas concentrations — CO₂, CH₄, and N₂O measurements from the Global Monitoring Laboratory.

Global Carbon Project

Comprehensive data on CO₂ emission sources, absorption sinks, and the global carbon cycle powering our emissions charts.

IPCC AR6

Warming trajectories, carbon budget estimates, and climate projections based on the latest IPCC Assessment Report.
