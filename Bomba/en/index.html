Bomba

The Best Price Near You

Find where to refuel for less! Real-time prices with official DGEG data and weekly forecasts to save money on fuel.

Features

⛽

Real-time Prices

View updated prices from all petrol stations in your area. Diesel, gasoline 95, gasoline 98, premium diesel or LPG.

🗺

Interactive Map

Find petrol stations around you on a map with color-coded markers: green for cheapest, orange for average, and red for most expensive.

📈

Weekly Forecasts

Know if prices will rise or fall next week! Receive a notification on Friday with the weekly forecast.

🔔

Push Notifications

Get price forecast alerts to plan your refueling at the best time.

📍

Direct Navigation

Tap on a station to see details and start navigation directly in Apple Maps or Google Maps.

🔒

Complete Privacy

Bomba works 100% on your device. No personal data collected, no account, no registration, no ads.

Data Sources

DGEG

Prices are obtained directly from the official database of the Directorate-General for Energy and Geology, ensuring reliable and up-to-date information.
