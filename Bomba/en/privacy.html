Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Bomba ("we", "our", or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

Data We Collect

Location Data

Bomba uses your device's location exclusively to show fuel stations near you. Your location:

  • Is processed only on your device
  • Is never sent to our servers
  • Is never shared with third parties
  • Is not stored or logged

Price Data

Fuel price data is obtained from the public DGEG (Directorate-General for Energy and Geology) API. This data is public and contains no personal information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

Bomba does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personally identifiable information
  • Location history
  • Usage data
  • Device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Third-Party Services

DGEG

We use the DGEG API to obtain fuel prices. No personal data is shared with this service.

Apple Maps / Google Maps

When you choose to navigate to a station, the app opens Apple Maps or Google Maps. Use of these services is subject to their respective privacy policies.

Data Storage

All preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using secure system storage. No data is sent to external servers.

Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there are no security risks associated with storing or transmitting your data.

Children's Privacy

Bomba does not collect data from any users, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised update date.
