Lightroom2Photos

From Lightroom to Apple Photos

Migrate your Adobe Lightroom Classic catalog to Apple Photos. Transfer photos, albums, keywords, ratings, and GPS data with a guided 4-step wizard.

Features

📂

Full Catalog Import

Reads your Lightroom Classic .lrcat catalog directly. No export needed, no intermediary formats.

📁

Album Migration

Recreates your Lightroom collections as Apple Photos albums, including nested folder hierarchies.

🏷️

Keyword Transfer

Writes Lightroom keywords into image metadata via ExifTool so they appear in Photos and Spotlight.

⭐

Ratings & Labels

Preserves star ratings and color labels by embedding them in image descriptions.

📍

GPS & Dates

Transfers GPS coordinates and original capture dates so your photos stay organized on the map and timeline.

🔄

Undo Migration

Made a mistake? Roll back the entire migration with one click. Imported photos and albums are cleanly removed.

🔍

Duplicate Detection

Importing into an existing library? Skip duplicates automatically based on filename matching.

🛡️

Safe & Private

Everything runs locally on your Mac. No cloud uploads, no accounts, no data leaves your machine.

How It Works

1

Select Your Catalog

Open your Lightroom Classic catalog file (.lrcat). The app reads it directly without modifying anything. Grant Photos library access when prompted.

2

Review & Configure

See a summary of your catalog: number of photos, collections, and keywords. Grant access to source photo folders, choose what metadata to transfer, and configure album options.

3

Migrate

Watch the progress in real-time as each photo is processed. Metadata is written via ExifTool, then photos are imported into your Photos library and assigned to albums.

4

Review Report

See a detailed report with success rate, imported/skipped/failed counts, and any warnings. Open Photos to verify, or undo the migration if needed.

Tips

Use a Separate Library

For safety, consider creating a new Photos library first: quit Photos, hold Option while opening Photos, and click "Create New". You can merge libraries later.

Install ExifTool

For keyword and metadata transfer, install ExifTool via Homebrew: brew install exiftool. Without it, photos are imported but keywords won't be embedded.

Requirements

  • macOS 15.0 or later
  • Adobe Lightroom Classic catalog (.lrcat)
  • ExifTool (optional, for metadata)

FAQ
