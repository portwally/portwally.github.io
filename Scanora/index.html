Understand Health Scores

Scanora displays easy-to-understand health and environmental scores

Nutri-Score
A B C D E

The Nutri-Score rates the overall nutritional quality of food products from A (best) to E (poorest), helping you make healthier choices at a glance.

üè≠ NOVA Processing Level

NOVA classifies foods by processing level from 1 (unprocessed natural foods) to 4 (ultra-processed). Choose less processed foods for better health outcomes.

üå± Eco-Score

The Eco-Score measures environmental impact from A to E, considering carbon footprint, packaging, transportation, and sustainability factors.
