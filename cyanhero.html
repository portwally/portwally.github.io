Supported Alternative Processes

Optimized presets for popular UV printing techniques

Cyanotype

Classic Prussian blue prints. Preset curve optimized for UV-sensitive iron salts.

Van Dyke Brown

Rich brown tones. Curve adjusted for silver-based emulsion response.

Platinum/Palladium

Archival noble metal prints. Two preset curves for different paper types.

Gum Bichromate

Multi-layer color printing. Optimized for dichromate sensitivity.

Photopolymer

Modern polymer gravure. Curve for precise exposure control.

Custom Curves

Load and save your own .acv curve files for any process.
