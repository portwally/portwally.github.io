About

A beautiful macOS app for extracting color palettes from images with powerful photo editing and transform tools. Watch the magic as colors are sampled from your images with animated visualizations. Built with SwiftUI and love.

Extract Colors, Create Palettes, Edit Photos

A complete color workflow in one beautiful interface. Extract palettes, apply themed filters, adjust curves, and explore your images with professional tools.
