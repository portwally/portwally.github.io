Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Your Privacy is Protected

Cores do Amor is designed with privacy in mind. The app runs entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Cores do Amor operates completely offline and locally on your Mac. The app:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect usage data or analytics
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not access your contacts, calendar, or other personal data
  • Does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies

Images You Process

When you open images in Cores do Amor:

  • Images are processed entirely on your device
  • Images are never uploaded to any server
  • Images are never shared with third parties
  • The app only accesses files you explicitly choose to open

Scene Detection Feature

Cores do Amor includes an AI-powered scene detection feature that uses Apple's Vision framework. This analysis:

  • Runs entirely on your device using Apple's built-in machine learning
  • Does not send images or analysis results to any external server
  • Is processed locally using Apple's secure on-device AI

Data Storage

Cores do Amor does not store any data beyond your current session. When you close the app:

  • Temporary image data is cleared from memory
  • No files are saved without your explicit action (Save button)
  • No preferences or settings are sent to external servers

Third-Party Services

Cores do Amor does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions completely independently without any external connections.

Children's Privacy

Cores do Amor does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over the images you process, and they remain entirely on your device.
