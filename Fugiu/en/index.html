Fugiu!

The Portuguese fisherman's companion

Real-time sea conditions, solunar activity, legal minimum sizes and your personal catch log — all in one app, no ads, no account, no tracking.

Features

🌊

Sea Conditions

Wave height, swell, period, direction and wind for 24 spots along the Portuguese coast, from Caminha to Vila Real de Santo António. 48-hour chart.

💧

Reservoirs

Water level and species for the main Portuguese reservoirs: Alqueva, Castelo de Bode, Montargil, Maranhão and Aguieira. Direct link to SNIRH.

🌙

Solunar Activity

Day rating based on moon phase. Best major and minor fishing windows with a live countdown to the next one.

📏

Rules & Closed Seasons

Minimum sizes and closed seasons for dozens of species. Quick checker — know instantly if you can keep that fish.

🔔

License Reminders

Notifications 30, 7 and 1 day before your sport, coastal, deep-sea or freshwater licence expires.

🎣

Catch Log

Record every fish with photo, size, weight, species and GPS location. Monthly and per-species stats, stored only on your device.

Data Sources

IPMA

Marine forecasts and station observations from the Portuguese Institute for Sea and Atmosphere.

Open-Meteo Marine

Swell, wave and wind data for the Portuguese coast, refreshed hourly.

SNIRH

Portugal's National Water Resources Information System — reservoir storage levels.
