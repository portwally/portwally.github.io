Technology

Shallow Water Equations

The simulation solves the 2D shallow water equations using an HLL Riemann solver with hydrostatic reconstruction for stability.

Natural Earth Data

Coastline and bathymetry data from Natural Earth public domain geographic datasets for accurate wave interaction.
