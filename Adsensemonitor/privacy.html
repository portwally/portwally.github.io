Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

AdSense Monitor ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google Account Data

When you sign in with your Google account, we access your basic profile information (name, email address, profile picture) and your AdSense/AdMob account data (earnings, impressions, clicks, payments). This data is accessed via the official Google APIs and is used solely to display your ad revenue information within the app.

2.2 Authentication Tokens

OAuth authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using Apple's Keychain. These tokens are used to authenticate API requests to Google services on your behalf.

2.3 Local Cache

The following data is cached locally on your device to improve performance:

  • Earnings reports and dashboard data
  • App and ad unit information
  • Payment history
  • Account settings and preferences

This cached data is stored only on your device and can be cleared at any time from the app's Settings.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

AdSense Monitor does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your Google account password
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage tracking or behavioral data

All communication happens directly between your device and Google's servers. We do not operate any backend servers or intermediary services.

4. Third-Party Services

AdSense Monitor uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0: For secure authentication with your Google account. You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Google AdSense Management API v2: To retrieve your AdSense earnings and payment data.
  • Google AdMob API v1: To retrieve your AdMob app earnings, impressions, and ad unit performance.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the app. Google may collect and use data for personalized advertising, including device identifiers and usage data. You can learn about ad personalization settings at adssettings.google.com.

5. Data Storage and Retention

All user data is stored locally on your device:

  • Authentication tokens: Stored in Apple Keychain until you sign out.
  • Cached reports: Stored temporarily for performance; cleared on sign-out or manually via Settings.
  • User preferences: Stored until you uninstall the app.

All data can be deleted by signing out within the app or by uninstalling the app.

6. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All API communication uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication tokens are stored in Apple's secure Keychain
  • We use OAuth 2.0 — your Google password is never shared with the app
  • No data is synced to cloud services or third-party servers

7. Children's Privacy

AdSense Monitor is intended for use by Google AdSense and AdMob account holders, who must be at least 18 years old per Google's terms of service. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Deletion: Clear cached data from Settings or sign out to remove all stored data
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke the app's access to your Google account at any time via myaccount.google.com/permissions

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
