AdSense Monitor

Ad Revenue Tracker for Mobile

Track your Google AdSense and AdMob earnings directly from your iPhone. View daily revenue, monthly trends, app breakdowns, and payment history — all in one place.

Features

📊

Real-Time Dashboard

See your earnings for today, yesterday, this month, and last month at a glance with clear, color-coded cards.

📈

30-Day Trend Chart

Track how your ad revenue develops over time with an interactive trend chart showing daily earnings.

📱

App-Level Insights

See exactly how each of your apps contributes to total revenue with per-app earnings, impressions, and ad unit details.

💳

Payment History

Keep track of past and upcoming payments with amounts, dates, and statuses — no need to open the web console.

💱

Multi-Currency Support

Earnings are displayed in your account's native currency, automatically detected from your AdMob account.

🌍

14 Languages

Available in English, German, Spanish, Italian, French, Portuguese, Dutch, Chinese, Polish, Swedish, Norwegian, Finnish, Korean, and Japanese.

How It Works

1

Sign In

Log in securely with your Google account using OAuth authentication.

2

View Earnings

Your AdSense and AdMob data loads automatically into a clean, intuitive dashboard.

3

Analyze & Optimize

Drill into detailed reports, filter by date range, and identify your top-performing apps.

Data Sources

Google AdSense API

Connects directly to the official Google AdSense Management API v2 to retrieve your website earnings and payment data.

Google AdMob API

Uses the official Google AdMob API v1 to fetch your mobile app earnings, impressions, clicks, and ad unit performance.

Requirements

  • iOS 18.0 or later
  • Google AdSense or AdMob account
  • Internet connection
