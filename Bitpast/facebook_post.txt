Ever wonder what your photos would look like on a Commodore 64? Or an Amiga 1200 in HAM8 mode?

BitPast lets you find out. 🕹️

It's a free, native macOS app that converts any image into authentic retro computer graphics — complete with real file formats you can load on actual hardware or an emulator.

✅ 15 classic platforms: C64, Amiga 500/1200, Apple II, Apple IIgs, ZX Spectrum, Atari 800/ST, BBC Micro, MSX, IBM PC, VIC-20, TRS-80 CoCo and more
✅ 15+ dithering algorithms (Floyd-Steinberg, Atkinson, Bayer…)
✅ Exports authentic native formats — .D64, .ADF, .SHR, .KLA, .SCR and 20+ more
✅ Drag & drop, batch processing, live preview
✅ Free & Open Source (MIT)

Whether you're a retrocomputing enthusiast, pixel artist, or just nostalgic for the 8-bit era — BitPast is made for you.

Attention retro computing fans, pixel artists, and homebrewers! 🎮

BitPast — a free, native macOS app — converts modern images into the authentic graphic formats of your favourite classic computers:

🍎 Apple II / IIgs (DHGR, HGR, 3200-color SHR)
💾 Commodore 64 (HiRes, Multicolor, Koala)
🖥️ Amiga 500 / 1200 (HAM6, HAM8 — up to 262,144 colors!)
⚡ ZX Spectrum, Amstrad CPC, BBC Micro
🕹️ Atari 800 / ST, IBM PC CGA/EGA/VGA, MSX/MSX2, VIC-20, TRS-80 CoCo

Export to real disk images (.D64, .ADF, .ATR, .ST, .DSK…) and load them straight into your emulator or real hardware.

Built with Swift on macOS. MIT licensed. No subscription. No nonsense.

🌐 Site: https://portwally.github.io/Bitpast/
