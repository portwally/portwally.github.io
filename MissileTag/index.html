Key Features

Everything you need for friendly warfare

📱

Real-time Multiplayer

Firebase-powered live sync. Both players see the projectile in flight, trajectories on the map, and impact resolution in real-time.

🔥

Hot Streak System

Every hit advances your weapon one stage. Chain hits to go from rocks to ICBMs in a single session. One miss resets everything.

🛡️

Defense Systems

Deploy Iron Dome shields, activate bunkers, send decoys, or just run. Multiple ways to survive an incoming strike.

📍

GPS Precision

Combat-grade location tracking during engagements. Everyday weapons need a 2-meter direct hit — basically standing on top of them.

🗺️

Live Missile Map

Satellite view with glowing trajectories, animated projectile dots, and blast radius circles. Track in follow-cam or full overview mode.

🍺

Beer Ledger

Automatic beer debt tracking. Miss a shot? You owe a beer. It's all tallied up on the friends screen. No escaping your debts.

Stake Modes

Play your way — from casual to competitive

🍺

Beer Mode

Miss a shot? You owe a beer. Track IOUs in a shared ledger. Social accountability built in. The default way to play.

🎯

Points Mode

Free-to-play with virtual currency. Leaderboards, bragging rights, and rank progression. No real-world stakes.

💰

Crypto Mode

Micro-stakes with ETH, SOL, or USDC. Smart contract escrow holds stakes until impact. Coming in v2.
